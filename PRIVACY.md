# XENOKING Auto Lister — Privacy Policy

XENOKING Auto Lister is a browser extension that loads a dealership's vehicle
inventory and prefills Facebook Marketplace vehicle listing forms.

## What we collect

- **Account data:** your email address, display name, and a password (stored
  only as a bcrypt hash) — used solely to sign you in and let the tool owner
  manage access.
- **Usage data:** which vehicles you have posted (VIN, title, price) so the
  tool can mark them as already listed.

## What we do NOT do

- We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
- We do not collect your Facebook credentials or read your Facebook messages.
- We do not track your browsing. The extension only acts on the Facebook
  listing pages you open and the dealer inventory you load.

## Where data lives

Account data is stored on the tool's own backend server. Inventory data and
posting history are stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage). Vehicle
inventory itself is public dealership listing data.

## Data removal

Ask the tool owner to delete your account at any time; this removes your
account data from the backend. Uninstalling the extension removes all locally
stored data.

Questions: contact the extension publisher via the Chrome Web Store listing.
